## Overview

Catppuccin Macchiato sits between Frappé and Mocha in Catppuccin's family
ramp — darker than Frappé but with more saturation than Mocha. The base
canvas is `#24273a`, slightly cooler than Mocha's near-black. Upstream
palette ships under MIT at github.com/catppuccin/palette.

## Colors

Mauve (`#c6a0f6`) is the canonical Macchiato accent — a touch more
saturated than Frappé's mauve. Lavender (`#b7bdf8`) is the active state;
pink (`#f5bde6`) plays the secondary-accent role.

The three-layer dark stack: `crust` (`#181926`), `mantle` (`#1e2030`),
`base` (`#24273a`). Body text reads as `text` (`#cad3f5`).

## Typography

Inherits feinschliff's typography unchanged — Catppuccin defines color only.
