## Overview

Catppuccin Latte is the lightest of Catppuccin's four flavors — daylight
pastel theme with a soft cream surface (base `#eff1f5`) and warm body ink
(`text` `#4c4f69`). Upstream palette ships under MIT at
github.com/catppuccin/palette.

## Colors

Mauve (`#8839ef`) is the Catppuccin Latte accent — slightly more saturated
than Mocha's mauve. Lavender (`#7287fd`) is the pressed state. Pink
(`#ea76cb`) plays the secondary-accent role.

Surface layers stack from lightest (`base` `#eff1f5`) through `mantle` and
`crust`, with progressively deeper `surface0/1/2` for emphasized elements.

## Typography

Inherits feinschliff's typography unchanged — Catppuccin defines color only.
